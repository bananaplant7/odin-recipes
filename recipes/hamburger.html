Hamburger

Description

This delicious hamburger recipe is perfect for a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • beef patty
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • mayonnaise
  • ketchup
  • pickles
  • onions
  • sesame seed buns
  • patience

Steps

  1. Slice tomatoes and onions
  2. Toast buns and grill patty
  3. Spread ketchup and mayo on the buns
  4. Place tomatoes, onions, lettuce, pickles, and the patty on the bun
  5. Wait a couple of days
  6. Enjoy
